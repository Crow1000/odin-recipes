Tacos in a Bag

Description

This is a kid friendly, camp friendly take on walking tacos!

While this does take the same prep work as the normal taco dish a.e. cooking the meat of choice and toppings, the clean-up can be easier and can be more fun to make with kids on the go as it can be made on the move, no plates required!!


Ingredients

  • One pound lean ground beef

  • One (1 ounce) package taco seasoning

  • 4 (2.5 ounce packages corn chips

  • 2 cups shredded lettuce

  • 1 chopped fresh tomato

  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

  • 1/3 cup salsa

  • 1/2 cup sour cream

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Place ground beef in a large skillet. Cook and stir over medium heat until browned. Drain excess oil. Mix in the taco seasoning and prepare according to the directions on the package.


  2. Step 2

    3. With the bags unopened, gently crush the corn ships. Snip the corners off the bags using scissors and slit open the bags alon the side edge. Spoon equal amounts of the beef mixture, lettuce, tomato, Cheddar cheese, salsa, and sour cream into the bags ontop of the crushed chips. Serve in the bag and eat using a fork.


Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 902 calories; Protein 34.6g; Carbs 54g; Fat 60.9; Cholesterol 134mg; Sodium 1390.1mg
